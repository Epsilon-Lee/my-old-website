Welcome to My GitHub Pages.

I am Epsilon Lee. I come from Chengdu, Sichuan, China, the hometown of panda and beautiful natural sceneries. Part of the web pages may about my life and others about my research, computational linguistics, machine learning and artificial intelligence.

The philosophy of writing about things is two-fold to me.

  1. As a Cognitive linguist John R. Taylor said in his book Cognitive Grammar, 'Thought is chaos before you put it into language.' So, recording things make us aware of past experience, and could think about future on more nurtured ground.
  2. Writing for sharing and resonating in thought and soul.
