Attention over tree and attention over attention, structural composition.

Today I will briefly comment on 3 papers newly updated on arXiv. Their names are listed as follows.

  • Neural Tree Indexer for Text Understanding
  • Attention-over-Attention Neural Networks for Reading Comprehension
  • Neural Semantic Encoder
It should be mentioned that the first and third papers are from the same researcher,Tsendsuren Munkhdalai. And the second one is from a former student of my lab, Yiming Cui. Neural Tree Indexer is proposed as a recursive neural network with flexible composition operation, i.e. the author dubbed it as $$n-ary$$ structure.
